About Me

Hi There!

I am Engr. Ghulam Ali, a passionate Full Stack Web Application Developer & DevOps Engineer with 4+ years of professional experience. I specialize in building scalable, secure, and high-performance applications while ensuring smooth deployment pipelines and cloud infrastructure management.

On the development side, I work with PHP (Laravel), JavaScript (React, Vue.js, Angular, Next.js, Nuxt.js), Node.js (Express), and Python (Django/Flask). For databases, I have hands-on expertise in MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Firebase, and Redis.

I am self-motivated, hardworking, and disciplined, with a strong problem-solving mindset. Beyond work, I enjoy listening to music, cooking, and reading books, which keeps me balanced and creative.

Whether it’s crafting a modern web application or orchestrating a complete DevOps workflow, I am committed to delivering high-quality solutions that truly make an impact.
